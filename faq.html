How Zakat is Calculated

A simple four-step process.

1. Add your assets Include cash, bank balances, metals, investments, business inventory, receivables, and other zakatable holdings.
2. Subtract short-term debts Deduct liabilities currently due. Long-term debt is generally not fully deducted.
3. Compare with nisab Check whether your net zakatable wealth reaches the chosen nisab threshold.
4. Pay 2.5% If above nisab after a full lunar year (hawl), Zakat due is 2.5% of net zakatable wealth.
